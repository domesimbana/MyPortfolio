HackMIT 2024

________________________________________

Earlier this year, I had the chance to attend HackMIT 2024: 24-hours of building, learning, and collaboration. My team and I created Maily, a smart mailbox system that uses a Raspberry Pi 5 and webcam to digitize community flyers. We built a full pipeline from image capture to text scraping to a website that recommends local events.

It was an exciting challenge that pushed us to learn quickly, apply full-stack skills under pressure, and think creatively about real-world impact. I walked away with sharper problem-solving instincts, a deeper appreciation for rapid prototyping, and a reminder that great tech starts with understanding the people it's built for.
